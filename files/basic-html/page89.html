Common Error 6.2  Is the negative form correct?

                                                              should not water
                 When there is a water shortage, homeowners do not should water their plants every day.
                    do not have to water
                 You have not to water some plants more than once a week.


                 RemembeR:  For negative single-word modals, put not between the modal and the base form of the
                              verb. For phrasal modals, add do, does, or did.
